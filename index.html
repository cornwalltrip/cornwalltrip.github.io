Autumn Dates Confirmed – Sunday, 17th October – The Dartmouth

Peter has progressed with the plans for our 2021 Tour, which will now take place on Sunday, 17th October at The Dartmouth Hotel, Golf & Spa, with the usual 3 nights/ 4 rounds format. Sunday tee time is confirmed as 12:05pm. Monday tee times are 10:05 followed by a further 9 holes on the second course at 15:00 for those interested (still standing). Tuesday will feature an away round at Dainton Park, and Wednesday’s tee time is confirmed as 9:05. Cost will be £340 for those playing golf, and £285 for those who want to come along and offer constructive criticism from the sidelines. Costs include all accommodation, meals and the all important prize kitty!

Please could you confirm with Peter that you’re able to join us.

Tee Times for Dorset “Minor” Tour

For those joining Peter in May, please note that the tee times are as follows: Monday at 13:24, Tuesday at 10:44, and Wednesday at 10:17.

2020 Tour Announcement

Leave a reply

A Message from Peter

To my very good friends,
With the C virus likely to peak at/during/near to our proposed visit, and the certainty that some of our group will not be present for very good reasons, I have bitten the bullet and arranged with Your Golf Travel (our agents) to postpone the trip for 12 months.
This will mean that deposits/payments already made will not be lost. Of less importance, it means we also secure more advantageous tee times for next year.

New date for 2021 is Sunday, 16th May. Full running order to follow.
In the meantime we have those who have paid a deposit – who will not be affected – and others who have paid in full. If this is you, please let me know if you would like a refund/part refund or whether you are happy to leave things as they are for the interim.
In the short term I will play things by ear in respect of the planned away-day on 18th April  and hope that something can happen – probably on a golf-only basis.
Also, and depending on how things pan out, I will look for a get together over one weekend in August/September.
Stand by for more news.
All the best, and please take care.

Peter

Autumn Away Day Report & Birthday Bash 2020!

Leave a reply

Whittlebury Park – Sunday, 29th September, 2019

The September away day took place under stressful circumstances not helped by a gloomy weather forecast that led to some absentees. The forecast proved to be disappointingly accurate and the predicted rainstorm and high winds arrived as play took place. Winner on the day was Mike Stannard with 29 points who also took nearest the pin on the 5th hole. Martin Sykes struck the longest drive of the day.

Birthday Bash!!

Looking ahead into 2020, we’re looking forward to the annual “Birthday Bash” that will take place this year at West Berks Golf Club on Sunday, 16th February with a first tee time of midday (meet at 11am). The cost for golf and prize kitty is a very reasonable £20. Please let Peter know if you’re able to attend and please note that there is no requirement for up-front payment on this occasion.

We hope to see as many of you there as possible.

Whittlebury Park – Sunday, 29th September 2019

Our final away day of the year will see a return to Whittlebury Park where we have been assured of buggy availability and fine course conditions.

First tee time is 10:00am and the cost is £25 including coffee and bacon roll on arrival. The cost to hire a buggy is £25.

Whittlebury Park have asked for payment in advance, so if you’re able to join us please transfer funds to the normal Harris Golf trip account asap. Peter will send a text with the account details for those who have misplaced it.

Finally, a small update regarding our May trip next year – a 9 hole “Woodland Walk” has been added to the itinerary for Monday with a tee time of 4:30pm. Perfect for those who would otherwise spend the entire afternoon and evening in the bar – yes Phil, that’s you!!

Summer Away Day Report – 21st July 2019

Leave a reply

South Cerney Golf Club

The day was once again hosted by Dave Mitchell and saw some fine scoring with Frank Tanner winning with 39 points closely followed by Paul Needle with 37 points.

No team event was held, but spot prizes were awarded as follows:

Nearest the Pin on the 15th – Dave Mitchell

Nearest the Pin in 2 on the 12th – Paul Needle

Congratulations also to Reg Brown who recorded the only birdie 2 of the day.
