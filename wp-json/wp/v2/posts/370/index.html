South Cerney Golf Club<\/h3>\n

Once again Dave Mitchell hosted our summer away-day at South Cerney. Eleven of us enjoyed some warm sunshine and an aerial display from the International Air Tatoo at nearby Fairford.<\/p>\n

The team game was the dreaded “Yellow Ball” where competitors have the added stress of letting their team mates down rather than just themselves.<\/p>\n

The team prize was won by Sue, Geoff, Reg and our host – Dave M with a score of 105 points. Worthy winners as they were the only team to\u00a0return the Yellow ball safely.<\/p>\n

Individual winner on the day was Sue with 37 points. Christine was runner up with\u00a0the same score but\u00a0a paltry\u00a019 points on the back nine!<\/p>\n

Sue also secured a birdie 2 on the long Par 3 6th hole. This feat was made more remarkable by the flypast of Spitfires and Hurricanes that accompanied it.<\/p>\n
