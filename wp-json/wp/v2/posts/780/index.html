West Berks Golf Club, Sunday 17th February<\/h3>\n

This year’s birthday event was held on a dry but chilly day. Individually, there were some reasonable scores and birthday boy Nigel Wain triumphed with a score of 38 points. This was followed by three players on 33 points who were separated on ‘count back’ in the following order: Mike Standard, Reg Brown & Peter Harris.<\/p>\n

Team wise, Team 3 (Nigel, Mike, Jane and Steve M) took 1st prize with 78 points. Team 2 (Richard, David W, Reg) scored 74 points and Team 1 (Peter, Mike Burton & Martin) scored 69 points.<\/p>\n

Spot prizes were awarded as follows:<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin on the 11th – Mike Stannard<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin in 2 on the 12th – David Wain<\/p>\n

Longest Drive on the 18th – Richard Edmonds<\/p>\n

Ladies longest drive – Jane.<\/p>\n

There was one birdie 2 recorded – Peter Harris.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
