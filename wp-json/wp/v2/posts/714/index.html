Rye Hill Golf Club<\/h3>\n
A select group gathered for the final away day of 2017, held on an old stamping ground (for some) at Rye Hill on Sunday 1st October.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Champion on the day was Richard Edmonds with a score of 32 points closely followed by Dave Scott with\u00a031 with Peter Harris in bronze position with 26.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
With the 2 shot reduction for winner(s), Richard is now playing off a handicap of\u00a04. Quite how he will cope on\u00a0future days remains to be seen.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
The team event went to team B. Richard, Dave ,Paul Needle & Sue Scott with 71 points.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
