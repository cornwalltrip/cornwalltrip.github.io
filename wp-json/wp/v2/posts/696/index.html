West Berkshire Golf Club<\/h3>\n
The second away day of the year was held in warm and dry conditions\u00a0on a course that is an old favourite for our group.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Scoring was not as high as at the birthday bash, with the victor carding 35 points. Winner Peter Harris became the second participant after Richard to take the new two shot handicap reduction . <\/div>\n

<\/div>\n
