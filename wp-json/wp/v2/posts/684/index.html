New regime for managing handicaps<\/h3>\n
2017 will see the introduction of a new mechanism for managing handicaps.<\/div>\n
Starting with the handicaps applied\u00a0at\u00a0Carswell on the 19th February, the following rules will apply:<\/div>\n
Your handicap\u00a0cannot be higher than your official Club handicap.<\/div>\n
Your handicap will be reduced by 2 shots if you\u00a0win.<\/div>\n
Your handicap will be reduced by 1 shot if your\u00a0score is within 1 of the winning score.<\/div>\n
Your handicap will increase by\u00a01\/2 a shot if your score is 8 points less than the winning score.<\/div>\n
Your handicap will increase by 1 shot if your score is 16 points less than the winning score.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
