Whittlebury Park Golf Club, Sunday 7th April<\/h3>\n

The April away day was held on the Whittlebury and Grand Prix (Red & Blue) nines at Whittlebury Park.<\/p>\n

The top two teams of Team 1 (Dave S, Sue, Paul N) and Team 3 (Richard, Steve C, Martin) could not be separated and so honours were shared. The other team comprised Peter H, John G and Mike S.<\/p>\n

Individually, Steve C triumphed with 37 points followed by Dave S (35) and Sue S (32).<\/p>\n

Spot prizes were awarded as follows:<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin on the 11th – John G<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin on the 5th – Mike S<\/p>\n

Longest Drive (9th) & Nearest the Pin x2 (18th) – Dave S<\/p>\n

No birdie 2s were recorded.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
