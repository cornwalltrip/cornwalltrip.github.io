Whittlebury Park – Sunday, 29th September 2019<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Our final away day of the year will see a return to Whittlebury Park where we have been assured of buggy availability and fine course conditions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

First tee time is 10:00am and the cost is \u00a325 including coffee and bacon roll on arrival. The cost to hire a buggy is \u00a325.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Whittlebury Park have asked for payment in advance, so if you’re able to join us please transfer funds to the normal Harris Golf trip account asap. Peter will send a text with the account details for those who have misplaced it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Finally, a small update regarding our May trip next year – a 9 hole “Woodland Walk” has been added to the itinerary for Monday with a tee time of 4:30pm. Perfect for those who would otherwise spend the entire afternoon and evening in the bar – yes Phil, that’s you!!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
