Tuesday, 17th May – Yelverton<\/h4>\n
The 2016 Open Championship was held at a new venue\u00a0for most of the attendees this year.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Overall, the scoring was good with more than\u00a0half the field scoring 30 points or more.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Out first, defending champion Robert Austen found the course to his liking setting a commendable target of 36 points . In his group Dave Scott came up one point short despite scoring 23 points on the back nine. Closely behind came Pete Cosford and Nigel Wain on 34 .<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Notable outward scores came from Nigel with 22, Rob with 21, Sue Scott with 19 and Bruce Mercer with 18.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Coming back, \u00a0Dave led the way with 23 with Richard 19 and Pete C 18.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Overall a victory for Rob retaining the trophy and being the first holder of the “Harris Hat of Glory”. This year, in a striking red colour.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n

Rob being congratulated by our host – Peter.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n
