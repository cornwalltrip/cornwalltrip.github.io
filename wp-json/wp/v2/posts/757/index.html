St Pierre – Mathern Course, 21st May (pm)<\/h3>\n

The green staff, having recovered enough to resume duties, had been hard at work and the course was presented in much improved condition. A number of players dropped out of the afternoon’s competition to allow themselves sufficient time to re-visit the enormous buffet that St Pierre had laid on for us. It was quite something to behold – the buffet that is, not Phil’s stomach after his 4th visit… The traditional afternoon competition format was Greensomes, and although a detailed record of events is unavailable, the day concluded with the “Mathern” team a further point ahead.<\/p>\n

St Mellons Golf Club, 22nd May<\/h3>\n

The Open championship was held at a sunny and very well presented St Mellons with champion Nigel Wain leading the way with a respectable score of 32 points.<\/p>\n

This was only good enough for fourth place behind Dave Scott 33 (out in 20 pts), Sue Scott\u00a0 36 and our new champion – keeping the trophy in the family – David Wain on 38.<\/p>\n

Team-wise the prize went to team “B” comprising the Davids mentioned above along with Sue Pritchard & Phil Reay with a total of 86 points (50 on the front nine).<\/p>\n

They were closely followed by team “A” of Nigel, Paul East, Richard Edmonds & Sue Scott with 84 points. \u00a0The other teams scored in the seventies.<\/p>\n
