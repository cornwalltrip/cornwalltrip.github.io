Update and Handicap List as of July 24th.<\/h3>\n

As previously announced, we’re introducing a new\u00a0handicap mechanism and have received feedback on the proposed changes. The system\u00a0moving forwards\u00a0is as follows:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Tour handicaps cannot be higher than your official Club handicap.<\/li>\n
  • Winners of Away-Days or the Championship will have their handicap reduced by\u00a02.<\/li>\n
  • If your score in an Away-Day event or the Championship is more than 15 shots\u00a0less than the winning score, your handicap will go up by 1.<\/li>\n
  • The handicaps at the start of each tour will apply to the Championship, but handicaps for matchplay will be adjusted during the tour as normal (+1 for losing \/ -1 for winning).<\/li>\n
  • At the end of each season (after the final Away-Day), the handicaps will be reviewed and altered based on performance during the year.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
