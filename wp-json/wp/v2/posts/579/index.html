Cotswolds Golf Club, Chipping Norton<\/h3>\n

A warm, sunny day and a golf course in lovely condition greeted the 12 players who joined us for the last Away Day of 2015.<\/p>\n

We headed out in teams of 3 with the usual individual Stableford format and “best two scores per hole” as the team competition format. There were audible sighs of relief from some members that the dreaded “yellow ball” format was absent (yes, that means you, Steve & Martin).<\/p>\n

Obviously buoyed by the selected format, Steve Mulford and Martin Sykes – alongside Frank Tanner – secured the team victory with a very respectable 78 points.<\/p>\n

Frank was also our champion golfer on the day, with a solid 35 points. Runners up were Dave Scott with 33 points with both\u00a0Steve M and Sue Scott scoring\u00a032 points.<\/p>\n

The point prizes were awarded as follows:<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin on the 3rd hole – Mike Stannard<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin in 2 on the 7th hole – Dave Scott<\/p>\n

Longest Drive on the 13th hole – Mike Stannard<\/p>\n

Nearest the Pin on the 15th hole – Steve Mulford.<\/p>\n

Details of the next Away Day will be posted on this site once details are confirmed – likely to be sometime in February, 2016.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
