Ogbourne Downs Golf Club<\/h3>\n
The July away-day found a new venue at Ogbourne Downs on Sunday 31st July, kindly hosted by Christine Mitchell.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
With no airshow or Open golf to distract the attendees, full focus could be applied to one’s own efforts.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
The Individual event was won by bionic David Mitchell with 35 points followed by Frank Tanner with 34 and Nigel Wain on 32.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Frank and David also scooped the team event in the company of Reg brown and Micky Hubbard.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Their score of 75 points eclipsed the team of Dave Scott, Richard Edmonds, Peter Harris \u00a0and the invisible man by one point – \u00a0with Dave& Keith Norman,Nigel and Steve Mulford a further single point back.<\/div>\n

and Keith Norman NTP 17th<\/div>\n
