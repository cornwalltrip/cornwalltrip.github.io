A Message from Peter<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

To my very good friends,
With the C virus likely to peak at\/during\/near to our proposed visit, and the certainty that some of our group will not be present for very good reasons,\u00a0I have bitten the bullet and arranged with Your Golf Travel (our agents) to postpone the trip for 12 months.
This will mean that deposits\/payments already made will not be lost. Of less importance, it means we also secure more advantageous tee times for next year.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

New date for 2021 is Sunday, 16th May. Full running order to follow.
In the meantime we have those who have paid a deposit – who will not be affected –\u00a0and others who have paid in full. If this is you, please let me know if you would like a refund\/part refund or whether you are happy to leave things as they are for the interim.
In the short term I will play things by ear in respect of the planned away-day on 18th April\u00a0 and hope that something can happen – probably on a golf-only basis.
Also, and depending on how things pan out, I will look for a get together over one weekend in August\/September.
Stand by for more news.
All the best, and please take care.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Peter<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
