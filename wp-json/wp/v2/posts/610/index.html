Wednesday, 18th May – Whitsand Bay<\/h4>\n
Recognising a lost cause when they see it, Wednesday saw the abandonment of the singles and replacement with a team “Pink Ball” challenge in groups of three.<\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
The sunshine of Looe was but a distant memory as the teams were confronted with weather that could be politely described as\u00a0changeable.<\/div>\n
In fairness, the heavy downpours were very intermittent and could hardly be blamed for\u00a0at least two of the groups losing\u00a0their ball on the first tee shot of the day.<\/div>\n
It may, however, have contributed to another group who chose to dispose of theirs towards the sea on the 6th.<\/div>\n
Results<\/h5>\n
The team of Sue Pritchard, Martin Sykes and ace team player Pete Cosford were oblivious to all this mayhem. They returned with pink ball intact, carding a commendable 106 points which was easily enough to secure victory.<\/div>\n
