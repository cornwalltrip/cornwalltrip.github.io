2013 Overview<\/h3>\n

The annual visit to Cornwall took place on the 19th – 23rd May and we were greeted with classic English May weather – unpredictable, windy and quite cold. However, we managed to avoid heavy rain, fog, sleet, snow etc.<\/p>\n

This year\u2019s team contest was once again won by the Christie team. Just as well, since the Christie team had forgotten to bring the trophy with them.<\/p>\n

This years open champion is Sue Pritchard who scored a fabulous 39 points around The Point at Polzeath. That score was matched by Pete Cosford who finished runner up by (only) scoring 21 points on the back nine!<\/p>\n

Final Scores<\/h4>\n

Christie 14.5 – 11.5 Craston<\/p>\n

The Open Championship:<\/h4>\n

Sue Pritchard – 39 points<\/p>\n
