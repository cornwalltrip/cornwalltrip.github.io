2014 Overview<\/h3>\n
\n

The annual visit to Cornwall took place on the 18th – 22nd May and I think it\u2019s fair to say we were quite lucky with the typical English May weather.<\/p>\n

Contestants for the championship at Lanhydrock were greeted with torrential rain, which somehow transformed into sunshine by the time we teed off!<\/p>\n

Sue Pritchard took advantage of the soft course conditions to successfully defend her title, and is 2014\u2019s champion golfer.<\/p>\n

One feature of this year\u2019s tour was the almost complete absence of record keeping!<\/p>\n

Another feature, by general consensus, was that 2014\u2019s tour was one of the most relaxing, convivial and downright enjoyable we\u2019ve had.<\/p>\n

The 1st day saw the Christie team get the upper hand, however this was completely reversed by the end of Monday\u2019s play that was dominated by the Craston team. A very close singles competition on the last day (4 – 4) was not enough for the Christie team to retain the trophy.<\/p>\n

The 2014 champions are the Craston team.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n
