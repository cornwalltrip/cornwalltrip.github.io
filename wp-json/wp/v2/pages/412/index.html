2012 Overview<\/h3>\n

The annual visit to Cornwall took place on the 20th – 24th May and we were greeted with fine weather that got warmer during the week. This year we added an additional day to the tour so that we could take part in David Wain\u2019s Captains Day event at Bigbury.<\/p>\n

This year\u2019s team contest was once again a very close affair, but this time the Christie team emerged victorious thanks in no small part to the return of their Captain himself. You can read how the story unfolded in the Day Reports.<\/p>\n

This years open champion is Derek Craston who scored an amazing 42 points around Carlyon Bay. Derek\u2019s surprise at his own victory was matched only by that of Mr Harris, who had luckily picked out Derek\u2019s name in the draw the night before.<\/p>\n

Final Scores<\/h4>\n

Christie 14 – 13 Craston<\/p>\n

The Open Championship:<\/h4>\n

Derek Craston – 42 points<\/p>\n
