      Another high scoring event with Richard Kidner winning the day with 41 points. Honourable mention should also be made of John Gent who score 36 points but missed out on third place following a count-back. The team game was everybody\u2019s favourite – Yellow Ball – and many dreams of victory were dashed on the par three 8th which is surrounded by water!<\/p>\n

      Nearest the Pin was won by Peter Harris. Nearest the Pin in 2 was won by Richard Kidner and the Longest Drive was won by Richard Edmonds. The all important 2\u2019s sweep saw money being passed to Nigel W, John G and our host Mr Harris (twice).<\/p>\n

              Nearest the Pin was won by Christine Mitchell (she succeeded where all else failed – to hit the green). Dave Scott clinched the Nearest the Pin in two and Martin Sykes won the longest drive.<\/p>\n

              Thanks are due once again to Dave M for hosting the event and in special mention must be made of the excellent meal we received afterwards.<\/p>\n

              Congratulations are due to our champion on the day – Martin, although the handicap committee are considering whether Martin should have a special \u2018South Cerney\u2019 handicap applied as this is not the first time he\u2019s played an excellent round of golf at this venue. Special mention must also be made of Frank Tanner for achieving a \u2018hole-in-1\u2019 at the 10th. A feat appreciated by all in the bar afterwards – thanks for the drink Frank!<\/p>\n
