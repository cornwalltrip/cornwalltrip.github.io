Away Day – Sunday, 19th April, 2015

West Berks Golf Club

A select band of eight players attended the day at West Berks.

The team event was won by messrs D Scott, G Scott, R Edmonds and R Brown with a score of 87 points easily vanquishing their opponents Harris, Gent, Sykes and Sue Scott, who did have the honour of a team member John Gent making a birdie at the 11th.

Reg Brown prevented Dave clearing up on the spot prizes by taking the newly introduced nearest-the-pin-in-3 on the 13th, otherwise, the nearest-the-pin-in-2 on the 3rd, nearest-the-pin on the 7th and longest-drive on the last were Dave’s!

In the individual event, Dave and Richard both started well with 19 and 21 points on the outward 9, but challenges coming home saw them each record 33 points with Dave edging into 2nd place on countback.

The winning score of 36 points was recorded by Peter Harris out in 20 and back in 16. Can this form last for a month until the main event in Cornwall? Only time will tell…..

Individual Event

  1. Peter Harris – 36 pts
  2. Dave Scott – 33 pts
  3. Richard Edmonds – 33 pts

Team Event

  1. Dave, Geoff, Reg, Richard – 87 pts
  2. Peter, Martin, Sue, John – less pts
