Autumn Away Day Report & Birthday Bash 2020!

Leave a reply

Whittlebury Park – Sunday, 29th September, 2019

The September away day took place under stressful circumstances not helped by a gloomy weather forecast that led to some absentees. The forecast proved to be disappointingly accurate and the predicted rainstorm and high winds arrived as play took place. Winner on the day was Mike Stannard with 29 points who also took nearest the pin on the 5th hole. Martin Sykes struck the longest drive of the day.

Birthday Bash!!

Looking ahead into 2020, we’re looking forward to the annual “Birthday Bash” that will take place this year at West Berks Golf Club on Sunday, 16th February with a first tee time of midday (meet at 11am). The cost for golf and prize kitty is a very reasonable £20. Please let Peter know if you’re able to attend and please note that there is no requirement for up-front payment on this occasion.

We hope to see as many of you there as possible.

Whittlebury Park – Sunday, 29th September 2019

Our final away day of the year will see a return to Whittlebury Park where we have been assured of buggy availability and fine course conditions.

First tee time is 10:00am and the cost is £25 including coffee and bacon roll on arrival. The cost to hire a buggy is £25.

Whittlebury Park have asked for payment in advance, so if you’re able to join us please transfer funds to the normal Harris Golf trip account asap. Peter will send a text with the account details for those who have misplaced it.

Finally, a small update regarding our May trip next year – a 9 hole “Woodland Walk” has been added to the itinerary for Monday with a tee time of 4:30pm. Perfect for those who would otherwise spend the entire afternoon and evening in the bar – yes Phil, that’s you!!

2019 Birthday Event

Leave a reply

West Berks Golf Club, Sunday 17th February

This year’s birthday event was held on a dry but chilly day. Individually, there were some reasonable scores and birthday boy Nigel Wain triumphed with a score of 38 points. This was followed by three players on 33 points who were separated on ‘count back’ in the following order: Mike Standard, Reg Brown & Peter Harris.

Team wise, Team 3 (Nigel, Mike, Jane and Steve M) took 1st prize with 78 points. Team 2 (Richard, David W, Reg) scored 74 points and Team 1 (Peter, Mike Burton & Martin) scored 69 points.

Spot prizes were awarded as follows:

Nearest the Pin on the 11th – Mike Stannard

Nearest the Pin in 2 on the 12th – David Wain

Longest Drive on the 18th – Richard Edmonds

Ladies longest drive – Jane.

There was one birdie 2 recorded – Peter Harris.

Spring Away Day Report – 8th, April 2018

Leave a reply

West Berks Golf Club

11 brave souls endured poor weather with both individual and team competitions held.
Individually, Nigel Wain (reigning tour champion) took the honours with 32 points including a back nine of 20 . Dave Scott carded 30 to finish runner up with Christine Mitchell third with 29.
Team wise, Nigel along with Richard Edmonds,Peter Harris and David Mitchell triumphed on count back with 64 pts from Christine, Sue Scott & Frank Tanner.
The other team – Dave, Paul Needle, Reg Brown and John Gent – also managed a score containing the numbers 6 & 4, however they were in a slightly different order.
Two spot prizes went to Paul who secured Longest Drive on the last and NTPin x2 on 3rd.
Sue was the longest lady (drive wise), and Nigel claimed NTP on the 11th. Nigel also secured the 2’s club money on the tricky 17th.
Next away day will be sometime in July tbc – but more importantly the main event looms in May.
It looks like Nigel’s coming into form at just the right time to defend his trophy.
