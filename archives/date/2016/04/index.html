Spring Away Day Report – 17th April, 2016

Leave a reply

The West Berkshire Golf Club

With mild and calm conditions coupled with a “damp” course scoring was respectable with over half the field scoring 30 points or more.
Coming out on top was Paul Needle with 35 points followed by Richard Edmonds 33 and Nigel Wain 32.
Thankfully no countback was needed!
Team wise Paul, along with Dave Scott and Geoff Scott, triumphed with 69 points.
The Spot prizes went to-
Pete Cosford NTP @ 7th
Peter Harris  NTP x 2 @ 12th
Dave Norman NTP @ 14th
Paul Needle  Longest Drive at the last.
Not content with his prize haul, Paul also swept up the 2’s club.
The next away day – subject to David Mitchells fitness/invitation – will be on “Open Sat/Sun”  in July.
More information to follow.
Onwards to Cornwall…

2016 Trophy

Leave a reply

The Masters has the Green Jacket, The Open has the Claret Jug.

This years competitors in the Cornwall Trip Open Championship will be battling for the honour of wearing the Harris Hat of Glory.

The 2016 Harris Hat is a striking red number and all our players will no doubt strive to raise their game in order to win the honour of wearing it with pride this year and beyond.
